About ARIANNA

From Vulnerabilities to Verified Control.

ARIANNA was founded with a clear mission: to bring structure, clarity, and continuous control to vulnerability management in complex and safety-critical environments.

As industries become increasingly connected, organizations face mounting regulatory pressure, long product lifecycles, and intricate supply chains. Traditional IT-centric security tools often fall short in these environments. ARIANNA was created to bridge that gap.

"Security is not a one-time effort — it is an ongoing responsibility."
ARIANNA

Our Vision

How We See Vulnerability Management

We believe vulnerability management should not be reactive, fragmented, or purely compliance-driven. It should be:

  • Continuous
  • Lifecycle-oriented
  • Structured and traceable
  • Designed for both IT and embedded/OT environments

Our vision is to enable organizations to manage cybersecurity risks systematically, from development through deployment and long-term operation.

What We Do

ARIANNA provides a structured platform for vulnerability identification, tracking, monitoring, and reporting. We help organizations:

  • Gain visibility into software and component risks
  • Monitor vulnerabilities across complex supply chains
  • Support secure development and DevSecOps processes
  • Maintain compliance with industry regulations and standards
  • Produce audit-ready documentation with confidence

Our approach combines technical depth with operational practicality, enabling teams to integrate vulnerability management seamlessly into existing workflows.

Built for Critical Industries

ARIANNA is designed for industries where reliability, safety, and compliance are non-negotiable — including automotive, industrial automation, medical devices, aerospace, defense, and other connected systems.

We understand the challenges of long product lifecycles, certification constraints, and embedded environments. Our solutions are tailored to meet those realities.

Our Commitment

At ARIANNA, we are committed to continuous innovation, transparent processes, regulatory alignment, and supporting our customers as long-term partners.

We work alongside organizations to ensure vulnerabilities are not just detected, but managed in a controlled, documented, and sustainable way.
